About Talon

Talon builds the habit.

Not a tracker. A system that delivers one small action at a time.

How it works

01

Pick a goal

Choose from four life categories.

02

Receive an action

Specific. Finishable. Generated for you.

03

Complete it

Mark it done. The next one builds on it.

04

Build the habit

What was a decision becomes a rhythm. The habit forms quietly, action by action.

Four categories

Physical
Body, movement, sleep, recovery.
Emotional
Stress, anxiety, confidence, presence.
Financial
Saving, earning, intentional spending.
Learning
Reading, skills, deliberate practice.

Why we built it

We had read the books. Started the routines. Quit them.

The gap between knowing what to do and actually doing it kept showing up — and nothing on the market closed it.

So we built what we wished existed. A system that takes the planning off the table, every day. One that does the work of designing the next small action — so the only thing left is doing it.
