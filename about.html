About Talon

Talon Builds You.

Not a tracker. A system that delivers one small action at a time.

Why We Built It

We read the books and started the routines. Then we quit them, every time.

The gap between knowing what to do and actually doing it kept showing up. And nothing on the market closed it.

So we built what we wished existed. A system that takes the planning off the table and hands you the next small action, so the only thing left is doing it.

Built to Win
How It Works

Four Moves. One Loop.

I

Pick a Goal

Choose what you're building toward in one of four areas of your life.

II

Receive an Action

One action, generated for you. Small enough that you can't talk yourself out of it.

III

Complete It

Mark it done. The next one builds on it.

IV

Build the Habit

What was a decision becomes a rhythm. The habit forms quietly, action by action.
